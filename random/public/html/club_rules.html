🏆 Weekly Game Club Rankings

Welcome to our Game Club leaderboard! Every week, we track who’s showing up, playing hard, and climbing the ranks. Here’s how your weekly standing is calculated. It’s fair, fun, and keeps everyone motivated—even if you miss a day!

📊 How Do Weekly Ranks Work?

  • All players who play at least once in the week are included in the weekly ranking.
  • Each day, if you play, we add your actual rank (1st, 2nd, etc.) to your weekly score.
  • If you don’t play on a day, a penalty score is added instead. 😬

❌ What’s the Penalty for Missing a Day?

If you skip a day, your penalty is equal to:

Number of players who played that day + 1

🧮 Final Score = Total of All Ranks + Penalties

The lower your total, the higher you rank! At the end of the week, everyone is sorted from the lowest score (best) to the highest (needs a comeback 😉).

💡 Some Fun Scenarios:

  • You play and win = 🥇 1 point
  • You come 5th out of 6 = 5 points
  • You don’t play and 6 others do = 7 points
  • You play all week = No penalties = You legend 🏅
